Oats

Description

Oats are really healthy and taste good when paired with the right ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Eggs
  • Water or milk
  • Blueberries
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey

  1. Mix the oats with eggs, blueberries and liquid of choice
  2. Put in microwave for 2 minutes
  3. Remove and mix. Repeat until hardened, but not solid.
  4. Add cinnamon and honey and Mix
