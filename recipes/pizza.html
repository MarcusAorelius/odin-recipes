Pizza

Description

This is the finished product.

Pizza might not be very healthy, but it is tasty.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pepperoni
  • Mince
  • Lots of cheese
  • Dough

  1. Spread the Dough
  2. Cook and mash the tomatoes
  3. Spread the tomato puree on the Dough
  4. Cover with toppings and cheese
  5. Put it in the oven at 200C until brown on the top
