Lasagna

Description

This is the finished product.

Lasagna might not be very healthy, but it is tasty.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Mince
  • Lots of cheese

  1. Cook and mash the tomatoes
  2. Spread pasta sheets on a tray
  3. Mix tomato puree and mince and pour it on top of the sheets
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Repeat the last 2 steps until you use all the ingredients
  6. Put it in the oven at 200C until brown on the top
